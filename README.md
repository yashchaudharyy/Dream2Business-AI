## 🧠 Problem Statement

Aaj ke time me logon ke paas **ideas bahut hote hain**,  
lekin unhe **business me convert karna** mushkil hota hai because:

- Proper roadmap nahi hota  
- Budget planning unclear hoti hai  
- Market research missing hoti hai  
- Execution strategy weak hoti hai  

---

## 💡 Solution

**Dream2Business AI** ek **Multi-Agent AI System** hai jo kisi bhi raw idea ko:

✅ Structured Business Plan  
✅ 30-Day Execution Roadmap  
✅ Budget & Financial Strategy  
✅ Market Insights  
✅ Risk Analysis  

me convert karta hai — within seconds ⚡

---

## 🤖 How It Works

System multiple AI agents ka use karta hai:

- 🧠 **Strategy Agent** → Business model & roadmap  
- 📊 **Market Agent** → Target audience & trends  
- 💰 **Finance Agent** → Budget & cost breakdown  
- ⚠️ **Risk Agent** → Challenges & solutions  

👉 Sab agents milkar ek **complete business ecosystem** banate hain

## 🛠️ Tech Stack

- **Python** – Core logic  
- **Streamlit** – UI/UX  
- **Gemini API** – AI Brain  
- **Pydantic** – Data validation  
- **MCP Architecture** – Agent communication
